📋 Quick Start Guide

  1. Click "Open Main Application" button above
  2. Enter a city name: Mumbai, Delhi, Bangalore, or Pune
  3. Click "Search Providers" button
  4. You should see provider cards appear
  5. Click on a time slot to see the price change
  6. Click "Book Now" to make a booking
